Genius 2

Helps you memorize things.


Get rid of those paper flashcards. Genius organizes your information and helps you memorize it, asking you questions using a proprietary adaptive “spaced repetition” method. Use Genius to learn foreign language phrases, vocabulary words, law and medical school materials, religious texts, marketing points, mathematical formulae—anything you need to memorize.


What’s new in Genius 2?

  • Supports rich text and pictures.
  • Fixed time quiz mode.
  • Refined look and feel, with font size control and other options.
  • Has a smarter algorithm for keeping track of your performance.

Frequently Asked Questions

- Why is Genius asking me the same questions again, even though I just answered them right?
- That’s due to the adaptive spaced repetition feature, designed to refresh your memory precisely before you forget. Over time, Genius will hone in on those items with which you’re having trouble.

- Is there an online repository where people can share Genius documents?
- Yes, it’s at http://www.geniusfiles.info/. Also check out the forum at http://groups.yahoo.com/group/genius-talk.

- I'd like to import data from Excel, etc. What file format does Genius import and export?
- Genius imports and exports standard tab-delimited text files.

Tricks

  • During quizzing, you can hit Y and N as shortcuts for “Yes” and “No.”
  • Command-Return ends editing an item.
