Genius 2

Helps you memorize things.


Get rid of those paper flashcards. Genius organizes your information and helps you memorize it, intelligently asking you questions using a special “spaced repetition” method. Use Genius to learn vocabulary words, foreign language phrases, historical events, religious texts, legal definitions, formal speeches, marketing points—anything you need to memorize.


What’s new in Genius 2?

  • Supports multimedia information, such as images and rich text.
  • New preference settings for controlling text size and sound effects.
  • Uses a smarter algorithm for keeping track of your performance.
  • Refined look and feel.
  • Takes advantage of new technologies in Mac OS X.

Tips

  • During quizzing, you can hit Y and N as shortcuts for “Yes” and “No.”
  • Command-Return ends editing an item.

FAQ

Q. Why is Genius asking me the same questions again, even though I just answered them right?
A. That’s due to the adaptive spaced repetition feature, designed to refresh your memory precisely before you forget. Over time, Genius will hone in on those items with which you’re having trouble.

Q. I'd like to import data from Excel, etc. Can I do this? What file format does Genius import and export?
A. Yes, Genius imports and exports standard tab-delimited text files.

Q. Is there an online repository where people can share Genius documents?
A. Yes, it’s at http://www.geniusfiles.info/. Also check out the forum at http://groups.yahoo.com/group/genius-talk.

Q. Can you write a Windows version?
A. No, sorry.
